# Books

## Recommended Reading

### Node.js Fundamentals
- **"Node.js: The Complete Guide"** - Maximilian Schwarzmüller
- **"Beginning Node.js"** - Baston K. Williams
- **"Learning Node.js"** - Marc Wandschneider

### Advanced Node.js
- **"Node.js Design Patterns"** - Mario Casciaro
- **"Node.js High Performance"** - Diogo Resende
- **"Mastering Node.js"** - Sandeep Kumar Patel

### JavaScript Deep Dive
- **"JavaScript: The Good Parts"** - Douglas Crockford
- **"You Don't Know JS"** - Kyle Simpson (free online)
- **"JavaScript Patterns"** - Stoyan Stefanov

### Architecture & Best Practices
- **"Architecting Node.js Applications"** - Dev Marketer course
- **"Node.js Microservices"** - Derick Sooman
